# 08 Get Internship

Convert your preparation into internship success and build the next step of your career.

## Goals
- Gain Experience
- Learn Industry Tools
- Convert Internship to Full-Time Job

## Suggested Actions
- Start the internship with a growth mindset and ask for meaningful tasks.
- Learn the tools, workflows, and best practices used by your team.
- Document your contributions and collect feedback.
- Volunteer for extra responsibilities and show ownership.
- Build strong relationships with mentors and teammates.
- Ask about full-time conversion criteria and deadlines.

## Outcome
- Practical industry experience
- Exposure to real development workflow and tools
- Better odds of securing a full-time offer
